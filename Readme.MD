## Sky Island Muleteer
# A roguelike game based on Cormac McCarthy's Blood Meridia

This is a old school roguelike inspired game based on the novel Blood Meridian by Cormac Mccarthy. The game objective is to become a profitable muleteer, running supplies in the Sky Islands region of Arizona and Mexico during the mid 1800s. You must learn how to survive and protect your mules in the desert and in the rough frontier towns. 

The game attempts to use regionally appropriate flora and fauna, combined with characters and situations plausible in Blood Meridian. 

The initial inspiration for the game was from my friend Alex Hindle, who thought of the name of the game as a passing joke, and I thought of it as a great challenge and inspiration to build. 

The game was almost 100% vibe-coded with help from Claude Code, but the concept, theme, and storylines were all mostly my own (of course ultimately inspired by Blood Meridian).
