HOW TO SURVIVE

Movement

WASD / Arrow Keys - Move

SPACE - Wait a turn

. - Also wait

Actions

Bump into enemies to attack

Walk into towns to enter

Stand on water to drink

L + direction - Look

T + direction - Throw item

M - Mine ore vein

Menus

C - Contract board

I - Inventory (equip/use/sell)

S - Shop (in town)

? - This help screen

ESC - Close menu

Inventory Keys

E + number - Equip item

U + number - Use item

Number - Sell item (in town)

Survival

Keep mules watered at cenotes/creeks

Cactus hurts - avoid cholla

Canyons are dangerous - ambush risk

Mine ore veins for gold & silver

Press ESC to close

Press ESC to leave town

CONTRACT BOARD

Press number to accept, ESC to close

INVENTORY

E+# equip | U+# use | # sell (town) | ESC close

GENERAL STORE

Press number to buy, ESC to close
